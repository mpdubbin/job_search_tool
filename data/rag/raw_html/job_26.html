Values & Innovation


At Under Armour, we are committed to empowering those who strive for more, and the company's values - Act Sustainably, Celebrate the Wins, Fight on Together, Love Athletes and Stand for Equality - serve as both a roadmap for our teams and the qualities expected of every teammate.



 



Our Values define and unite us, the beliefs that are the red thread that connects everyone at Under Armour. Our values are rallying cries, reminding us why we're here, and fueling everything we do.



 



Our pursuit of better begins with innovation and with our team's mission of being the best. With us, you get the freedom to go further - no matter your role. That means developing, delivering, and selling the state-of-the-art products and digital tools that make top performers even better.



 



If you are a current Under Armour teammate, apply to this position on the Internal Career Site Here. 
Purpose of Role


We are seeking a dynamic Data Scientist to join our Enterprise Data Management & Analytics organization. This dual-focused role requires you to balance traditional data science responsibilities with strategic support for our Advanced Analytics & AI initiatives. You will spend half of your time developing and deploying machine learning solutions, while the other half will involve leveraging your expertise and internal knowledge to uncover high-impact opportunities, assess feasibility, and guide the implementation of innovative AI and analytics solutions.


Your Impact


    
	
  • Develop, maintain, and expand machine learning and AI solutions to support critical functions such as global price optimization and enterprise data management.
    • 
	
  • Utilize AWS cloud computing and MLOps best practices to deploy scalable models effectively.
    • 
	
  • Innovate with novel statistical and machine learning methodologies to tackle complex business challenges.
    • 
	
  • Develop deep expertise in our internal modeling approaches and translate technical insights into actionable recommendations for a diverse audience.
    • 
	
  • Continuously update your skills with emerging technologies and modeling techniques to drive technical excellence.
    • 
	
  • Work collaboratively to drive improvements through the lifecycle of each product to ensure the technical implementation appropriately aligns with business goals.
    • 
	
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to identify and evaluate high-impact opportunities across business units.
    • 
	
  • Act as a strategic partner by guiding the design and implementation of AI-driven solutions that align with evolving business objectives.
    • 
	
  • Work closely with the Advanced Analytics & AI teams to integrate cutting-edge generative AI techniques and advanced analytics methods into our operational framework.
    • 
	
  • Communicate effectively with technical and non-technical stakeholders to ensure clarity, alignment, and buy-in throughout the project lifecycle.
    • 



Qualifications


    
	
  • 3+ years of data querying languages, scripting languages or statistical/mathematical software experience OR Master’s degree with typically 1 year(s)+ of relevant experience. 
    • 
	
  • Proficiency with SQL and Python.
    • 
	
  • Ability to apply data science to real-world problems, capable of breaking down complex problems into key components solvable by machine learning solutions.
    • 
	
  • Knowledgable of limitations and best practices related to model specification, comparison, selection, and deployment.
    • 
	
  • Ability to provide written and oral interpretation of highly specialized terms and data, and ability to present this data to others with different levels of expertise
    • 
	
  • Prior experience working on an agile team preferred
    • 
	
  • Prior experience leveraging version control (e.g., Git, SVN) in a team setting preferred
    • 




 



Bonus Qualifications:



    
	
  • Ability to help guide deep-dive sessions to ideate production path to solutions.
    • 
	
  • Writing skills to create and deliver tutorials, newsletters and presentations for technical, business, and executive stakeholders.
    • 
	
  • Experience experimenting with current Generative AI tools from a variety of models. 
    • 
	
  • Prior experience with AWS services (e.g., Sagemaker, S3, ECR).
    • 



Workplace Location


    
	
  • Location: Fully Remote
    • 
	
  • Return to Work Designation: Fully Remote
    • 
	
  • Travel: 5% of the year 
    • 
	
  • Licenses/Certifications: N/A
    • 



Relocation


    
	
  • No relocation provided
    •

    • 
	
  • Under Armour Merchandise Discounts

    • 
	
  • Maternity and Parental Leave for eligible and FMLA-eligible teammates
    •

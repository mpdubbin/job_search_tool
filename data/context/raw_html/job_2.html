Data Scientist, See & Spray

Remote

Position Description

We’re Blue River, a team of innovators driven to create intelligent machinery that solves monumental problems for our customers. We empower our customers – farmers, construction crews, and foresters - to implement safer and more sustainable solutions, driving increased profitability with less reliance on scarce labor. We believe that focusing on the small stuff – pixel-by-pixel and task-by-task - leads to big gains. 

Blue River Technology aligns with John Deere’s vision to “innovate on behalf of humanity” by quickly identifying and solving high-value, high-uncertainty challenges in AI, machine learning, computer vision, and robotics. BRT acts as a research and development flywheel, building not only new products but also new platforms that reliably create value for both Deere and its customers. From fully autonomous machines to highly precise farming equipment, BRT and Deere are partnering to create technical breakthroughs in industries like agriculture and construction. 

Our people are at the heart of what we do. Through cross-discipline collaboration, this mission-driven team is eager to define the new frontier of robotics. We are always asking hard questions, rapidly iterating, and getting our boots in the field to figure it out. We won’t give up until we’ve made a tangible and positive impact on the planet! Blue River Technology is based in Santa Clara, CA.

This position is eligible to be fully remote in the US.

 

Summary

You will be a part of the See & Spray program. We are responsible for building the world’s most intelligent herbicide sprayers. By using data, either at pixel-by-pixel or plant-by-plant level, our team drives the innovations that go into the machine. We are looking for a talented Data Scientist to join our growing team. You’ll work on exciting data problems, collaborate with various stakeholders, and contribute to helping us solve monumental challenges in battling herbicide resistance and reducing overall chemical usage while ensuring our customers’ productivity output.

Job Responsibilities

A combination, not necessarily all-inclusive, of the following:

  • Analyze large datasets to provide actionable insights and guide business decisions.
  • Develop and implement statistical models and basic machine learning algorithms such as A/B testing, regression analysis, and clustering.
  • Work with SQL and PySpark to query and manipulate large datasets.
  • Provide ad-hoc analytics support to engineering and business teams and offer recommendations based on your analysis.
  • Collaborate with various teams to define metrics and data needs, implement data collection strategy, conduct analyses, and interpret findings to help set engineering and business priorities.
  • Create and maintain dashboards and visualizations to communicate data-driven insights effectively.
  • Translate complex data into clear, compelling stories for both technical and non-technical stakeholders.

Required Experience and Skills

  • Fluent in Python (preferred) or R: you should be comfortable working with Python/R for data analysis, statistics, and machine learning tasks.
  • Experience with SQL and Big Data Querying: proficient in writing efficient SQL queries and experience working with big data platforms (preferably PySpark).
  • Data Visualization & Storytelling: proficient in creating dashboards and visualizations (using tools like Tableau, Power BI, or Looker), and skilled at telling a compelling story from the data.
  • Knowledge of Statistics & Machine Learning Algorithms: a solid understanding of statistics and hands-on experience with A/B testing, regression, and clustering techniques.
  • Strong Communication Skills: ability to clearly explain complex analyses and insights to both technical and non-technical audiences.
  • Experience with Multiple Stakeholders: experience working cross-functionally with various teams to gather requirements and deliver data-driven insights.
  • 2 or more years of experience in a related field.

Preferred Experience and Skills

  • Interest in robotics and farming: desire to learn how smart robotic systems are transforming agriculture
  • Data Engineering Skills: Experience in building and maintaining production-level data pipelines.
  • C++: Familiarity with C++ is a plus for any advanced computational tasks or performance optimizations.
  • Familiarity with cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, Azure), Docker, and CI/CD pipelines is a plus.
